Schools that offer a cybersecurity specialization (in France)

A site allowing you to better inform yourself Onisep

> post-bac engineering schools

Here are the main post-baccalaureate engineering schools that will allow you to specialize in the fields of cybersecurity and cyber defense:

  • 3iL ,
  • l'ESIEA,
  • l'UTT (systems, networks and telecommunications specialty),
  • l'EPITA (issues a title of computer engineering expert in apprenticeship),
  • l'EISTI (computer engineering specialty),
  • Polytech Grenoble (specialty computer networks and multimedia communication),

> post-prep or post-bac +2 engineering schools

After a scientific preparation or after a BTS SIO here are the main post-preparation engineering schools and engineering schools in parallel admissions which will allow you to train yourself as an IT security expert:

  • l'ENSIBS, which has opened a new “cyber defense” specialty since the start of the 2013 school year.
  • l'ENSTA ParisTech,
  • Telecom SudParis,
  • l’EPSI,
  • l'ENSI Bourges (security and IT technologies)
  • l'ENSICAEN (IT specialty),
